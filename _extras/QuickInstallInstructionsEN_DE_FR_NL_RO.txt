English :

1) Create a folder "commodv2" in your mods/ directory on your server. If the mods/ folder doesn`t exist you
need to create this in the same location as the main/ and pb/ folders

2) Upload the files z_svr_v2_commod.iwd, z_v2_commod.iwd and mod.ff to the folder "commodv2" you created.

3) Start the mod from your servers command line.
